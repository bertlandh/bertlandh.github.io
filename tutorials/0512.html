Connecting Ouya Controller to Windows 10 in 2021

What You'll Need

  • An Ouya controller (obviously)
  • A Windows 10 PC with bluetooth

Pairing the Controller

  1. Press and hold the Ouya logo button on the controller for about 5 seconds, until the two center LEDs are flashing
  2. On the PC, right click the start button > Run
  3. Type devicepairingwizard > Press Enter
  4. The Ouya controller should show in the listing (may display as "Input device")
  5. Select it from the listing > Click Next
  6. Leave the passcode blank > Click Next
  7. After a few seconds, the controller should successfully pair with Windows
  8. Game
